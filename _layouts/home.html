Baftique — Empowering Women in Chitral 2024 – Present
  • Founded Baftique, a social enterprise committed to the economic upliftment and empowerment of women in Chitral, Pakistan, through sustainable livelihoods rooted in local culture and tradition.
  • Lead initiatives to preserve and promote the rich cultural heritage of Chitral, including traditional handcrafts, weaving, and indigenous art forms that are at risk of disappearing due to modernization.
  • Build partnerships and networks to connect Chitrali women artisans with broader markets, creating economic opportunities while honoring authentic craft traditions.
  • Advocate for women's rights and education in remote communities, working to break barriers and create pathways for women to achieve financial independence.
  • Write and publish articles on cultural preservation, women's empowerment, and the intersection of tradition and modernity in underserved communities.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: To uplift the women of Chitral by creating sustainable economic opportunities rooted in cultural preservation, education, and community empowerment.
  • Vision: A future where every woman in Chitral has access to the resources, skills, and platforms needed to thrive economically while keeping alive the traditions that define their identity.
  • Impact Areas: Economic empowerment, cultural heritage preservation, women's education, artisan skill development, and community advocacy.
